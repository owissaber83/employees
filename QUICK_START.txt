🚀 HOW TO USE:

  1. Open HR Management System
  2. Go to: Attendance & Departure page
  3. Find: Advanced Attendance Search (NEW!)
  4. Select employee and date range
  5. View results and summary
  6. Click "Generate Salary Report" for details
  7. Print or save as needed


📊 CALCULATION EXAMPLE:

  Employee: Ahmed Mohammed
  Monthly Salary: 5,000 SAR
  
  Daily Rate = 5,000 / 26 = 192.31 SAR/day
  Absence Days = 2
  Absence Deduction = 2 x 192.31 = 384.62 SAR
  Net Salary = 4,500 SAR
  Final Salary Due = 4,500 - 384.62 = 4,115.38 SAR
